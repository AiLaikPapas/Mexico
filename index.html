The beginning of Mexico history

The beginning of Mexico's history dates back to the arrival of the original settlers more than 10,000 years ago. Little by little great peoples began to emerge (the Olmec, Teotihuacan, Maya and Mexica or Aztec civilizations stand out) that occupied different and large territories and developed their civilizations.
